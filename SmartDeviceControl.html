Quick Start Guide / Help

Welcome to BabaALD IoT Dashboard

BabaALD is a real-time smart home control dashboard powered by Firebase. Control up to 6 IoT devices from any browser. All changes sync instantly to your ESP32 devices.

Adding a New Device

  • Select device type, enter a unique name, choose attributes.
  • Click Generate OTP, enter the OTP shown, click Verify & Save.
  • Maximum 6 devices allowed.

Live Control

Each device card shows a Switch On/Off toggle. Click a card to expand its full controls. Expanded card moves left; others stack on the right.

Managing Devices

Go to Manage Devices, select a device, update its attributes, then click Update Settings. To delete, use Delete Device with OTP confirmation.

Device Features

Smart Lamp

RGB Colour Mixer with presets (Warm White, Scarlet Red, Light Blue, Baby Pink, Orange, Violet, Green), Lighting Patterns (Default / Random / Heartbeat / Groove / Psychedelics / Mutate / Rotate), Night Mode.

Smart Fan

Speed Control (1-5), Timer, Temperature-Based On/Off, Auto Schedule.

Smart Speaker

Volume Control, Internet Radio Mode, Timer.

Smart Lamp with Alarm

All Lamp features plus Volume, Timer, and Alarm setup.

Smart Cooktop

Power Level (1-10), Timer, Temp-Based On/Off (triggers once at food temp), Auto Schedule.

Smart Fridge

Switch On/Off, Timer, Auto Schedule.

Smart Intrusion Detection

Sensitivity (0-100, higher = more sensitive), Email Alert with address input, SMS Alert with phone number input.

Tips

  • Keep device names short and descriptive (no special characters).
  • Hover over any control label to see a tooltip.
  • RGB knobs update the colour preview circle in real time.
