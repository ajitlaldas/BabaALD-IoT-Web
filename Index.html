ABOUT US

Welcome to BabaALD's World — a passion project born out of curiosity, late nights, and the sheer joy of making things work.

Who We Are

We are hobbyists. Not engineers by title, but engineers by heart. BabaALD's World is the digital home of a self-taught maker who woke up one day and decided that life is too short not to build stuff.

What We Do

We tinker. We prototype. We occasionally let the magic smoke out of components and learn from it. Our interests span a deliberately wide arc:

  • Microcontrollers & Embedded Systems — ESP32, Arduino, STM32 and anything with registers to poke.
  • IoT & Home Automation — Making lights obey, fans think, and fridges gossip over Wi-Fi.
  • Circuit Design — Schematic capture, PCB layout, and the satisfying moment a custom board boots for the first time.
  • 3D Modelling & Printing — Designing enclosures and mechanical parts so the electronics have a body to live in.
  • Web Development — Building the interfaces (like this one!) that let humans talk to machines.
  • Databases & Cloud — Firebase, real-time sync, and keeping things connected even when we're asleep.

Our Philosophy

This is not a startup. There is no venture capital, no pivot strategy, no monetisation roadmap. This is a human being keeping the excitement alive — learning new things, breaking things, fixing things, and sharing the journey with anyone who finds it interesting.

If even one person reads something here and thinks "I can build that too" — we've done our job.

CONTACT US
